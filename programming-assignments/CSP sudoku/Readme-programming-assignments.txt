Programación Assignment 1
Esta carpeta contiene la solución al problema de programación descrito a continuación. Se incluye el código fuente y el informe técnico correspondiente.

Enunciado de la tarea
You must design a Sudoku solver. For this problem, you should formulate it specifying the variables, their domains and constraints with their types (unary, binary, higher-order, global), analyzing the backtracking algorithm with alternative approaches that reduce the computation time, memory consumption, or lead to early detection of failing paths. You must only implement the approach that in your analysis is deemed more effective or efficient. For the algorithm implemented you must test and analyze it for at least three levels of Sudoku difficulty.
